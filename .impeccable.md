## Design Context

### Users

- This personal homepage serves mixed audiences: recruiters/interviewers, potential collaborators/clients, and peer developers/community readers.
- Primary jobs to be done:
  - Understand who you are and what you do quickly.
  - Evaluate your thinking quality through curated personal shares.
  - Reach out for collaboration with low friction.

### Brand Personality

- Personality keywords: sharp, intentional, trustworthy.
- Emotional goal: create high-contrast confidence first, then deepen interest through system-like detail and editorial pacing.
- Voice/tone: concise, human, grounded; avoids buzzword-heavy self-promotion.

### Aesthetic Direction

- Core direction follows `DESIGN.md` as the current source of truth:
  - Dark-only Terminal Architect system.
  - Obsidian surfaces with cyan / purple energy accents.
  - Monospace metadata and system-like labels.
  - Zero-radius geometry and restrained neon glow instead of soft editorial warmth.
- Motion preference: controlled and purposeful. Emphasize reveal choreography, focus states, and subtle hover feedback without flashy gimmicks.
- Anti-reference: avoid warm editorial parchment aesthetics, soft-radius SaaS polish, or any visual language that conflicts with the terminal/system identity.

### Design Principles

1. **System Clarity First**: each section should feel like part of a coherent command-center interface, not a collection of unrelated marketing blocks.
2. **Dark Surface Consistency**: colors, borders, and elevations should stay within the dark terminal hierarchy defined in `DESIGN.md`.
3. **Intentional Hierarchy**: use strong contrast between display copy, body copy, and monospace metadata so scanning feels immediate.
4. **Meaningful Motion**: animation should guide attention and reinforce structure, never distract.
5. **Composed Components**: prefer consistent component primitives and shared tokens over ad-hoc styling so future audits evaluate against one stable design system.
