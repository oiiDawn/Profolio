## Design Context

### Users

- This personal homepage serves mixed audiences: recruiters/interviewers, potential collaborators/clients, and peer developers/community readers.
- Primary jobs to be done:
  - Understand who you are and what you do quickly.
  - Evaluate your thinking quality through curated personal shares.
  - Reach out for collaboration with low friction.

### Brand Personality

- Personality keywords: warm, thoughtful, trustworthy.
- Emotional goal: create calm confidence first, then deepen interest through editorial pacing and refined details.
- Voice/tone: concise, human, grounded; avoids buzzword-heavy self-promotion.

### Aesthetic Direction

- Core direction follows the Claude-inspired system in `DESIGN.md`:
  - Warm parchment background and warm neutrals only.
  - Serif-led hierarchy with sans utility text.
  - Terracotta as the single high-signal brand accent.
  - Ring-based depth, soft radius, chapter-like light/dark alternation.
- Motion preference: rich but controlled. Emphasize smooth reveal choreography and hover micro-interactions without flashy gimmicks.
- Anti-reference: avoid generic template-like SaaS landing page visuals.

### Design Principles

1. **Editorial Clarity First**: each section must communicate one clear intent with generous spacing and readable rhythm.
2. **Warm Consistency**: all colors, borders, and elevations stay in warm-toned families; no cold tech palette drift.
3. **Intentional Hierarchy**: serif for narrative authority, sans for utility; maintain strict heading/body contrast.
4. **Meaningful Motion**: animation should guide attention and reinforce structure, never distract.
5. **Composed Components**: prefer consistent component primitives (Tailwind + shadcn) over ad-hoc styling to keep the system coherent.
